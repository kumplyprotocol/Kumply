# KUMPLY Product Marketing Context

## Product
**KUMPLY** — On-chain compliance infrastructure for the Avalanche ecosystem.

## What It Does
Issues encrypted, non-transferable eERC (Encrypted ERC) identity credentials (KYC, KYB, KYA) on the AVALANCHE® blockchain. Protocols can verify user compliance status on-chain without accessing personal data.

## Target Audience
1. **DeFi Protocol Teams** — Need compliance gating for institutional liquidity
2. **RWA (Real World Asset) Platforms** — Need KYB for tokenized securities
3. **AI Agent Developers** — Need KYA (Know Your Agent) for autonomous on-chain actors
4. **LatAm Fintech Companies** — Need LFPDPPP/GDPR-compliant identity verification

## Key Differentiators
- **Non-custodial**: We never store PII. Data is encrypted client-side.
- **Software-only**: We are NOT a VASP, exchange, or money transmitter.
- **Cross-L1 via ICM**: Verify once, read everywhere across Avalanche L1s.
- **eERC standard**: Encrypted ERC tokens for attestation — non-transferable, on-chain, privacy-preserving.
- **Sub-second finality**: Leverages Avalanche Snowman consensus.

## Primary Pain Point
DeFi protocols need regulatory compliance to onboard institutional capital, but existing KYC solutions are centralized, custodial, and incompatible with on-chain composability.

## Primary CTA
"Get Your Attestation" — Connect wallet → choose verification type → receive on-chain credential.

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional but accessible** (institutional audience, developer-friendly)
- **Technical but not jargon-heavy** (show expertise without alienating)
- **Confident, not promotional** (we state capabilities, not hype)
- **Security-first** (compliance, privacy, and trust are core themes)
